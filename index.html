Patterns from real winners

  1. Concentration beats diversification. #1 (ETH only), #2 (SOL only), #3 (SILVER), #4 (semis basket), #7/#8 (oil only). #9 had a balanced 5-asset basket — net +$175 after fees. The fees-vs-diversification trade-off destroys small accounts.
  2. HIP-3 markets dominate top ROI. 5 of top 10 are stock/commodity perps (oil, silver, semis). Crypto winners need either huge capital + months (#1 ETH, #2 SOL) or big trends.
  3. Crude oil (xyz:CL) is the most-traded high-PnL market. 3 of top 10 wallets concentrated on CL alone.
  4. Silver is the highest single-market PnL. 0x096e made $62K on SILVER alone over 5 months.
  5. Fees are the #1 killer at small size. #6, #7, #9 had >25% of gross gains eaten by fees.
  6. Win rate doesn't matter, R:R does. James Wynn (off-leaderboard) had 43.59% win rate, made $87M then lost most of it on conviction longs.

Strategies decoded from videos + on-chain

1. MoonDev's liquidation-momentum arb (transcribed from YouTube 2026-03-11)

Setup: watch live HL liquidations. When >$25K liquidates on one side (e.g., shorts get rekt), more liquidations on that side likely follow within 5 min (cascade). Take the same-direction trade. Hedge on Polymarket 5-min binary markets at uneven sizes (100 long crypto / 80 short binary) so the hedge isn't 1:1. Edge = liquidation cascade momentum + cross-venue mispricing.
Data needed: live liquidation feed (CoinGlass $900/mo OR HL public clearinghouseState polling).
Why it works: liquidations are forced sells/buys. Once a wave starts, market makers pull, slippage jumps, more liquidations trigger.
Capital floor: $15 per leg per his demo. Works at small size.

2. Single-stock concentration (decoded from 0xcf67…eb24)

Setup: +$47,849 in 17 days on xyz:MU, AAPL, INTC, HOOD. ~$520K account. ~988 fills. Fees only $290 (means almost all maker orders). Avg trade size ~$50K.
Why it likely works: retail traders use HL after-hours equity perps to react to news (earnings, Fed, geopolitical). Pricing lags real spot until US open. Whale rests bids/asks around the lag, captures the spread + the catch-up.
Capital floor: not retail-friendly — needs deep enough size to be a maker on these books.

3. Conviction long-hold (decoded from 0xc387… ETH wallet, TheWhiteWhaleHL)

Setup: levered long on ETH, hold for months through drawdowns. 8 months → +$137K on ~$244K account = +56% gross.
Why it works: if you pick the right asset and the right cycle phase, conviction beats trading. HYPE itself has 10x'd from launch to peak.
Capital floor: any size — but mental fortitude is the real bar (60%+ DD survival).

4. Single-commodity hyperfocus (decoded from oil + silver wallets)

Setup: all-in on one HIP-3 commodity (oil OR silver). 24/7 perp price drifts off NYMEX/CME spot during off-hours. Bot positions ahead of NY open.
Why it works: commodity traders globally use HL as their off-hours venue. Predictable rebalance flows.
Capital floor: small ($14K accounts in our top-10) — works at retail size.

Risks & what could go wrong

  • Survivorship bias. The leaderboard only shows winners RIGHT NOW. Most who tried these strategies blew up.
  • HIP-3 liquidity. NVDA perp had $5.8M total OI in Feb — one whale = 68% of book. Your $30 order moves price.
  • Earnings/event risk. Stock perps gap on earnings. 7-10% moves common. Liquidation in seconds.
  • Concentration risk. One bad trade on your one market wipes the account. The winners had ROOM to be wrong; we don't.
  • Oracle risk. HIP-3 prices come from oracles. Oracle outage = bot trading bad data.
